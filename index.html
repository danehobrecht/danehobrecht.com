Dane Hobrecht is a versatile actor known for his dynamic range and ability to infuse physical comedy into his performances. While he initially made a name for himself on stage, his film debut in 2023's Richard's Roast, where he played the leading role of Tom, has already garnered buzz and earned him a spot on the 2023 Sundance Film Festival roster.

Dane Hobrecht has also left a mark in several other notable productions, including Footloose!, Sweeney Todd: The Demon Barber of Fleet Street, and The Lightning Thief: The Percy Jackson Musical, earning critical acclaim for his performances.

Dane Hobrecht is currently nominated for most "Outstanding Supporting Performance in a Musical" by the Orange County Theatre Guild (OCTG), earning NoSquare Theatre in Laguna Beach its first-ever nomination. With his unique blend of comedic timing and dramatic range, Dane Hobrecht has firmly demonstrated his potential for exponential growth in the entertainment industry.
